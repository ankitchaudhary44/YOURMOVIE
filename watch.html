Ways to Watch YOURMOVIE on Different Devices

You can watch YOURMOVIE on any supported smart TV, phone, tablet, streaming player, or game console that connects to the internet and offers the YOURMOVIE app, or at yourmovie.com using a computer.

TVs and TV Streaming Devices

Many TVs, TV streaming devices, and media players come with the YOURMOVIE app included. To get started, use the links below to learn how to get YOURMOVIE on your device.

  • Amazon Fire TV/Stick
  • Apple TV
  • Chromecast
  • LG
  • Roku TV/Stick
  • Samsung
  • Sony
  • TCL
  • Vizio

Note: YOURMOVIE may no longer be available on some TVs and TV streaming devices made before 2015.

Mobile Phones and Tablets

You can watch YOURMOVIE on any supported mobile phone or tablet. To get started, use the links below to see specific requirements and learn how to get the YOURMOVIE app on your device.

  • Android phone or tablet
  • iPhone or iPad
  • Amazon Fire tablet
  • Windows tablet

Computers

You can watch YOURMOVIE on Mac, Windows, or Chromebook computers. To get started, go to yourmovie.com using a supported web browser, or get the YOURMOVIE app for Windows.

Cable Boxes

Many internet, cable, and pay TV providers include the YOURMOVIE app on their devices. To get started, search the name of your provider in our Help Center to check if YOURMOVIE is available on your device.

Video Game Consoles and AR/VR Devices

You can watch YOURMOVIE on these popular video game consoles and AR/VR devices. To get started, use the links below to learn how to get YOURMOVIE on your game console.

  • Sony PlayStation consoles
  • Microsoft Xbox consoles
  • Apple Vision Pro
  • Meta Quest headsets
